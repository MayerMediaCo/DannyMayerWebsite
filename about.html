My Story

My name is Danny Mayer and I am a freelance marketer, web designer, and blogger.

I developed a passion for marketing which started in highschool. I spent much of my time playing in bands around the local music scene. And as every young artist dreams, I tried getting my music out in every possible way, which lead me to learn about social media, the power of content, and the psychology behind marketing.

I was enthralled by it.

I went on to study marketing in college and learn front end web development as well, mainly doing projects for local bands and businesses as I seeked to increase my skills and gain experience in the field.
